    A well-known higher education college in Nepal "Birendra Multiple Campus" is connected to Tribhuvan University. With more than 40 years of expertise, we offer a variety of programs in education, administration, science, and the humanities. Our faculty is committed to delivering academic achievement and is highly qualified, diverse, and experienced. We support a comprehensive education that encourages social responsibility and personal growth. We give our students access to cutting-edge facilities, a safe atmosphere to learn in, and a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities and community involvement. We appreciate you thinking of Birendra Multiple Campus for your higher education need.
